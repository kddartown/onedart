This is the KDSmart Home Screen

Basic Trial details and options are displayed

You can scroll through the Trials from left to right

Select the Trials Button to view a list of all Trials and extra options

You can get to more options with the Navigation Bar on the left e.g. the Traits Screen

Or the Tags Screen

The Devices Screen provides options for connecting various devices e.g. Scanners

Settings are general settings for KDSmart

Trial details can be viewed from the Home Screen or the Trial Screen

Select the Home Button to get back to the Home Screen
