What is
KDDart?

A software suite that integrates phenotypic, genetic &  environmental data for breeding & multidisciplinary 
agro-ecological research.

KDXplore
Trial/Nursery selection data for breeders, technicians, curators and developers. 

KDSmart
Integrated mobile application for in field data collection & storage. 

KDCompute
Platform for extensible data analysis in an efficient and customisable framework.

KDManage
Administrative and day-to-day management data.

KDSens
Interface between various generic environmental sensors, such as weather stations, soil probes.
