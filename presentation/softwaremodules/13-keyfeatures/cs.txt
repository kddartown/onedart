Key Features

Genotype/Specimen (Germplasm/Accession)
Organise trials into plots and collect phenotypic data and other important information to those plots.

Marker Data
Store large marker datasets and link it to the stored Genotypes & allow full integration of marker data with phenotypic and environmental data. 

Data Search
Genotype data can be indexed and searched via KDDart and assist in selection and the analytic processes.
